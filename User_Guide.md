## üöÄ Getting Started

### Accessing the App
Simply visit the **Recipe Share Web Portal**. You can browse public recipes immediately, but to save favorites or post your own, you will need to sign in using your **UC credentials** via Auth0.

### Creating an Account
1. Click the **"Sign In"** button on the top right.
2. Select the **University of Cincinnati** login option (SSO).
3. Once authenticated, your profile will be automatically created.

---

## üîç Finding Recipes
The **Search Bar** at the top of the screen is powered by Elasticsearch, allowing you to find meals quickly based on:

* **Ingredients:** e.g., "Tofu, Broccoli"
* **Time:** e.g., "Under 20 mins"
* **Dietary Needs:** e.g., "Vegan", "Halal", "Gluten-Free"

## ‚úçÔ∏è Contributing a Recipe
Ready to share your culinary masterpiece with other Bearcats?

1. Click **"Post a Recipe"** in the navigation menu.
2. **Title:** Give it a catchy name.
3. **Ingredients:** List items clearly (e.g., "2 cups of rice").
4. **Steps:** Provide numbered instructions.
5. **Upload Photo:** Add a clear image of the final dish (Max 5MB).
6. **Submit:** Your recipe will be live and searchable instantly!

## üìñ Your Digital Cookbook
Found a recipe you love? Click the **Heart icon** or **"Save to Cookbook"** button.

* Access your saved list by clicking your **Profile Icon** and selecting **"My Cookbook"**.
* You can remove recipes at any time to keep your collection curated.
